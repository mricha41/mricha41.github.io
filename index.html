Project Cerberus

Our Motto

Highly usable, data-driven wireless systems improving personal interactions.

Goals

The purpose of this project is to discover ways wireless doorbell systems can maximize the quality of personal interactions through design.

Team Cerberus will perform research and design a prototype demonstrating how better design benefits consumers of wireless doorbell systems by meeting expectations and anticipating future needs.

Commitment to R&D

Team Cerberus is devoted to R&D. We believe that our efforts will benefit consumers in many ways, and constantly ask:

How can wireless doorbell systems be designed to leverage consumer interactions and information in the environment to provide more value to consumers?

Generally, we try to continuously answer the following questions throughout our research:


  • Who is visiting consumers at the door?
  • Why are they making a visit?
  • When do these visits occur?
  • What information is available in the consumer environment relevant to these interactions?
  • How do consumers use their wireless doorbell systems?
  • What kind of features do consumers expect their wireless doorbell systems to have?
  • How can wireless doorbell systems be designed to anticipate the future needs of consumers?

Online Survey

Cerberus conducted an online survey via SurveyMonkey. Data collection has closed for now, but the survey is still open if you'd like to take a look. However, any submissions will be ignored at this point. The raw data are available for the research community to analyze. Data are also available in the following formats:

  • Open Office
  • Excel

Data analysis was performed using R - the code is available for the research community to study.

If you'd like to peruse the survey instrument we used to collect data, you can find it in our research instrument repository here.

The results are in...and the public has spoken. Some support was found for our theoretical basis of personas in the structured interview preliminary research, which gives Cerberus a strong basis for marketing its products. The findings also suggest Cerberus consider the following features when developing the product:

  • Doorbell system silence setting
  • Local storage for doorbell system video/media

Sketches of the product we have under development are available. Thanks to research participants, the product is expected to have relatively strong viability in the market.
